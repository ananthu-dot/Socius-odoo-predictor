# Sales, Revenue, Product Demand, and Customer Forecasting Platform
## Project Overview

This project aims to develop a predictive analytics platform capable of forecasting revenue, sales activity, product demand, and customer behavior. The initial phase focuses on developing and validating time-series forecasting models using publicly available datasets before transitioning to company-specific data sources.

### The long-term objective is to provide actionable business insights that support:

Revenue planning
Inventory management
Sales forecasting
Customer retention strategies
Product demand optimization
Business Objectives
